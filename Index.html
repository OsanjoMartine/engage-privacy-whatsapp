Privacy Policy for Tingg-Engagement

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tingg-Engagement handles information in connection with our automated security and notification services. We act as a service provider for our business clients to facilitate secure communication with their customers.

2. Data Collection (Platform Data)

We obtain "Platform Data" exclusively through the Meta Platform APIs (WhatsApp Business API). This data is provided to us by our business clients (such as Simbisa or Tingg) to enable the delivery of specific messages. This information primarily includes:

  • Customer Phone Numbers: Used solely as the destination for automated messages.
  • Message Status: Information regarding whether a message was sent, delivered, or read.

Note: We do not use Facebook Login, and we do not collect personal profile data like gender, friends list, or birthdays.

3. How We Use Data

We use Platform Data to provide a seamless, 24/7 notification experience for our clients' users. This is strictly limited to:

  • Security Codes (OTP): Sending private, one-time security codes to verify identity during login or registration.
  • Activity Alerts: Sending instant notifications for payment confirmations, transaction receipts, and account updates.

We do not use this data for marketing, nor do we share it with third parties for advertising purposes.
